Best photo locations in Cyprus

Seven spots that deliver drama, colour, and Mediterranean light — from sea stacks to mountain ridges.

01

Petra tou Romiou (Aphrodite’s Rock)

Mythic sea stacks off the Paphos coast — long exposures turn the surf into silk, and sunset paints the limestone gold. Arrive early or late; midday is harsh on the rocks.

02

Cape Greco

Sea caves, arches, and cliff edges — the water reads cyan to deep blue. Great for wide-angle coastal shots and silhouettes at last light.

03

Blue Lagoon, Akamas

Glass-clear water and white cliffs — bring a polariser. From boats or the viewpoint above, the lagoon reads as a jewel against the green peninsula.

04

Troodos Mountains

Pine forests, villages, and ridgelines above the haze — fog in the valleys can add mood. Winter snow on the peaks is a rare bonus for Cyprus.

05

Paphos Archaeological Park

Mosaics, columns, and coastal light — structured compositions and leading lines. Golden hour softens the stone and deepens shadows.

06

Limassol Marina & old town

Reflective glass, rigging, and narrow alleyways — mix modern harbour with textured stone streets. Blue hour makes the water and lights pop.

07

Ayia Napa sea caves

Sculpted sandstone and churning water — dramatic foreground and endless horizon. Watch the waves; a tripod and patience pay off.
